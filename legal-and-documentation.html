Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our legal and documentation services

Do you help with rent or lease agreements?

Yes, we draft and register rental/lease agreements in compliance with local laws and your terms.

Can you help me register property documents or sale deeds?

Absolutely. We coordinate with legal professionals and local authorities to complete the registration process efficiently.

What if there's a legal dispute regarding the property?

Our legal partners can represent or guide you through property disputes, eviction notices, and court proceedings if required.

Do you assist with power of attorney for NRI clients?

Yes, we facilitate the execution and notarization of Power of Attorney documents for absentee landlords.

Is documentation available in digital format?

Yes, all legal documents are stored securely and shared digitally for easy access and record keeping.
